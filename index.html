About

Senior Mechatronics Engineering student at the Lebanese American University (LAU) with a strong academic record, hands-on engineering experience, and active involvement in tutoring and student leadership.

I work as an academic tutor for engineering and mathematics courses, supporting students with problem-solving, concept clarification, and exam preparation across multiple technical subjects.

Also, I served as Vice President of the Student Council at LAU–Beirut, where I coordinated student initiatives, represented student interests, and worked closely with university administration to improve campus life.

Recently, I completed a summer internship in the Energy Program at LAU–Beirut, focusing on sustainable facility management, HVAC systems, energy efficiency, and exposure to ASHRAE standards and building management systems.

I am currently working on a research project on programmable matter in collaboration between LAU (Lebanon) and UMLP (France), with an interest in building practical, well-modeled systems that bridge theory and real-world implementation, particularly in control systems, robotics, and automation.
